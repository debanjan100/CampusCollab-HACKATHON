ğŸ¯ About
CampusCollab solves the "talent silo" problem on campus by providing a centralized platform where students can:

Showcase their skills and what they want to learn
Find project partners with specific skill sets
Post projects and find teammates
Get academic help from peers
Build a reputation through endorsements

Think of it as LinkedIn for campus collaboration - but focused on peer-to-peer connections rather than jobs.

ğŸ“ Project Motivation
Built for the HACK-ULA Hackathon organized by ACM RVCE Student Chapter, CampusCollab addresses the critical problem of talent silos in educational institutions. Students often struggle to find peers with specific skills for:

Academic projects and assignments
Hackathon teams
Research collaborations
Club activities
Peer tutoring

Our solution creates a centralized, skill-based platform that breaks down inter-departmental barriers and fosters a culture of collaboration.
